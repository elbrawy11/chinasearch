ChinaSearch helps shoppers compare China marketplace deals before buying

ChinaSearch is a smart shopping-comparison platform built to help users discover products from Chinese marketplaces and compare offers by price, shipping speed, rating, country, currency, platform and estimated rewards.

Smart comparisonCompare product offers using price, shipping, rating and deal score signals.
Country awareShopping preferences, shipping expectations and currency can change by region.
Quick decision viewQuick View explains why a deal may be suitable before visiting the marketplace.
Transparent modelChinaSearch does not sell products directly. Purchases happen on the original marketplace.
Pre-launch transparency: Product data and rewards are currently used for demonstration and platform review. Official affiliate links will be connected only after marketplace or affiliate-network approval.

How ChinaSearch ranks deals

  • Price: lower price and stronger savings improve the deal score.
  • Shipping: faster and more suitable shipping improves the recommendation.
  • Rating: stronger ratings and reviews increase confidence.
  • Country fit: recommendations may adapt to the selected country and currency.
  • Estimated rewards: rewards are not guaranteed and must be confirmed by the partner after approval.
